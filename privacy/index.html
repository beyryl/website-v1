Beyryl Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

Beyryl (“Beyryl,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information when you visit our websites, request information, create an account, receive or accept an invitation, use Beyryl applications, platforms, or related services, or otherwise interact with us (collectively, the “Services”).

This Privacy Policy also explains your rights and choices with respect to your personal information.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites, services, or applications that are not operated by Beyryl, even if they are linked from our Services.

Customer data and organization-managed accounts

Beyryl is primarily a business service. In many cases, Beyryl processes information on behalf of a customer organization that provides access to the Services for its employees, contractors, or other authorized users.

If Beyryl processes personal information on behalf of a customer in connection with that customer’s use of the Services, Beyryl acts as a service provider or processor under the applicable customer agreement. In those cases, the customer organization controls how the relevant data is used and many privacy requests should be directed to that customer first.

If your employer, customer, partner, or other organization provides you with access to Beyryl, that organization may control your account, tenant settings, permissions, and certain content associated with your use of the Services.

Information we collect

We collect personal information that you provide directly to us, information we collect automatically when you use the Services, and information we receive from customers, partners, administrators, and other sources.

The information you provide directly to us may include your name, work email address, company name, job title, phone number, mailing address, support communications, survey responses, and any other information you choose to provide to us.

If you create an account, receive an invitation, or use an organization-managed account, we may collect account, profile, and identity information such as login identifiers, invitation status, account credentials or authentication metadata, organization or tenant identifiers, role assignments, and preferences.

If you or your organization purchase, subscribe to, or administer the Services, we may collect billing and transaction information such as billing contact details, subscription information, invoices, payment status, and limited payment-related metadata from our payment processors. Beyryl does not store full payment card numbers unless expressly stated for a specific workflow.

When you use the Services, we may collect customer content and operational data needed to provide the Services. Depending on the features in use, this may include tenant records, policy and governance artifacts, configuration settings, files, prompts, outputs, audit trails, deployment metadata, support tickets, workflow history, and other content submitted to or generated through the Services.

We also collect technical, diagnostic, security, and usage information such as IP address, browser and device information, timestamps, pages or features accessed, log files, performance metrics, crash or error data, approximate location derived from IP address, and security-related events.

We may use cookies, local storage, session identifiers, pixels, and similar technologies on our websites and in certain parts of the Services to remember preferences, maintain sessions, support authentication and security, measure performance, and improve functionality.

We may also receive information from customer administrators, partners, integration providers, service providers, and publicly available sources where necessary to create accounts, administer tenants, provide support, improve the Services, market our Services, or comply with law.

How we use personal information

We use personal information to provide, operate, maintain, administer, and support the Services.

We also use personal information to authenticate users, manage invitations and access controls, create and administer accounts and tenants, provision customer environments, process billing and subscription operations, respond to inquiries, provide customer support, and communicate with you about transactions, product changes, security notices, service updates, and events.

We use personal information to monitor performance, diagnose errors, investigate incidents, prevent fraud and misuse, protect Beyryl, our customers, and others, comply with legal obligations, and enforce our agreements and policies.

We may use personal information to improve, develop, analyze, and secure the Services, including through troubleshooting, product analytics, service reliability work, reporting, and internal operational review.

We may also create aggregated, statistical, or de-identified information and use that information for lawful business purposes, including analytics, security, service improvement, and reporting.

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing, Beyryl relies on one or more of the following: performance of a contract, legitimate interests, consent, and compliance with legal obligations.

How we disclose personal information

We may disclose personal information to our affiliates and related entities.

We may disclose personal information to service providers, contractors, and subprocessors that help us operate the Services and our business, including providers of hosting, cloud infrastructure, authentication, payment processing, email delivery, monitoring, observability, analytics, customer support, professional services, and security functions.

We may disclose personal information to customer organizations and their authorized administrators when accounts are created, provisioned, or managed on their behalf, or when those organizations are entitled to manage the environment in which the account is used.

We may disclose personal information to business partners or integration partners when you or your organization direct us to do so, or when such disclosure is necessary to provide an integrated feature or service you have chosen to use.

We may disclose personal information to professional advisors such as lawyers, auditors, insurers, and financial advisors where necessary in connection with professional services they provide to Beyryl.

We may disclose personal information to regulators, law enforcement, courts, or other third parties when required by law, subpoena, court order, or other legal process, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect rights, safety, security, or property.

We may also disclose personal information in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, bankruptcy, sale of assets, or similar corporate transaction.

Beyryl does not sell personal information.

Customer content and AI-related use

Beyryl may process customer content only as necessary to provide, secure, maintain, support, and improve the Services, and as otherwise permitted by applicable agreements and law.

Unless Beyryl clearly discloses otherwise in a product notice, customer agreement, or feature-specific workflow, Beyryl will not use customer content submitted through business Services to train general-purpose models for third parties.

Cookies and similar technologies

Beyryl may use cookies and similar technologies to operate our websites and Services, remember preferences, support login and security functions, understand usage, and improve performance.

You can often control cookies through your browser settings. If Beyryl provides a cookie banner, preference center, or similar choice mechanism, you may also manage your choices there.

Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including to provide the Services, maintain security, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, comply with legal obligations, and keep appropriate business and audit records.

Retention periods vary depending on the type of information, the sensitivity of the information, the feature or workflow involved, our contractual commitments, and applicable legal requirements.

We may retain archived copies, backups, logs, and de-identified information for longer where permitted or required by law.

Security

Beyryl uses technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

No method of transmission over the Internet or method of storage is completely secure, and Beyryl cannot guarantee absolute security.

International transfers

Beyryl may process personal information in the United States and other countries where Beyryl or its service providers operate.

When required by applicable law, Beyryl uses appropriate safeguards for cross-border transfers, such as contractual protections or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to certain processing, or receive a portable copy of your information. You may also have the right to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent and to opt out of marketing communications.

You may exercise marketing choices by using the unsubscribe link in a message or by contacting us using the details below.

If you want to exercise privacy rights, contact us using the contact details below. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

If Beyryl processes your information on behalf of a customer organization, we may direct your request to that organization or ask you to contact it directly.

Beyryl will not discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights available under applicable law.

Children

The Services are intended for business and professional use and are not directed to children. Beyryl does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to Beyryl, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to investigate and, where appropriate, delete the information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and revise the effective date above. If required by law, we will provide additional notice.
